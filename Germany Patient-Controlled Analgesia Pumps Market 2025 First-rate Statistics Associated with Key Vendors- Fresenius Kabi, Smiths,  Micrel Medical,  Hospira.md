Patient-controlled analgesia pumps, also referred to as PCA is one of the methods that is used to control the amount of analgesia (pain medicine) that patients receive. There are various number of patient -controlled analgesia systems. In patient-controlled analgesia, the patient can control their pain. This done with the help of patient-controlled analgesia pump. A PCA pump is a computerized pump that contains a pain medication syringe (as per prescription of the physician) that is directly connected to the IV (intravenous) line in the patient's body. The PCA pump, in certain cases, delivers a constant and small pain medication flow.
